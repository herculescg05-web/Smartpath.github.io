The problem

Commuting in Cebu shouldn't be this hard

Thousands of students and commuters face the same daily struggles with Cebu's jeepney system.

Confusing signboards

Jeepney route signs are unclear and inconsistent, leaving newcomers and students stranded and unsure which ride to take.

Late arrivals

No way to know when the next jeepney arrives means missed classes, delayed work, and wasted time standing at stops.

Overspending on fares

Wrong routes and unnecessary transfers cost students extra money they can't afford, every single day.

Features

Everything you need to commute smarter

SmartPath combines real-time GPS data, route intelligence, and simple design so anyone can navigate Cebu confidently.

Live GPS tracking

See exactly where each jeepney is in real time and know to the minute when it arrives at your stop — no more blind waiting.

Optimal route guidance

Just enter your destination and SmartPath tells you exactly which jeepney to ride, where to board, and where to transfer.

Automatic fare calculator

Know the exact fare before you board. No more overpaying or underpaying — SmartPath calculates it automatically per route.

Arrival time estimates

Live countdowns for every major jeepney stop so you can plan your departure time and never rush to catch a ride again.
